Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic paper-and-pencil game played on a grid of 3x3 squares, where players take turns marking X's and O's in an attempt to get three of their marks in a row, column, or diagonal.
Hangman

Hangman is a word-guessing game where players attempt to identify an unknown word by guessing individual letters within a limited number of chances before a stick figure representation is completed.
Connect four

Connect 4 is a strategy game where players take turns dropping colored discs into a grid, aiming to be the first to form a line of four discs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
